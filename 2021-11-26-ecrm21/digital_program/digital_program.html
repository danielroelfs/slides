Øystein Runde

Comic writer·Artist·Creator

The combination of entertainment and research is dear to Øystein Runde. After letting Henrik Ibsen and Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson fight oil-fuelled robot zombies in the steampunk graphic novel De Fire Store, and having Futen (Taxman), collect tax from literal trolls, Runde has since moved on to science communication: His fact book Stamceller (Stem cells) had Dagbladet write "a book that makes your kid smarter than you", and was picked to be in the UNs sustainability library. Øystein has since been hired to improve a PhD presentation, help NorPEPS in recruiting test subjects with an animated film, and rethinking the profile and communication of www.tissueengineering.no for Bergen Tissue Engineering. He will talk about the thoughts and perspectives and tools you need to turn a complex topic into something digestible, why experts go blind, why you always need to see the crime drama in your science, and what you should do if you can't afford to hire Øystein Runde.
